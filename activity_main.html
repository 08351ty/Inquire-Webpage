What is Quicksnap?

Quicksnap is an education app that allows primary and secondary school students to have access to the brightest university students in the world: those who have aced every secondary school curriculum, topped the SAT and ACT tests and are currently studying at world class universities.

Students

Stuck on a homework problem the night before the assignment is due? Finding it difficult to grasp the concept behind a theory that's being tested the following day? Simply take a snapshot of your problem and, within minutes, have a tutor from a top tier college answer and explain the solution. Replace your underqualified, overpriced and non-personalized tutors from various tutorial centers with Quicksnap, the best source of knowledge for all your academic problems.

Tutors

Worked hard throughout to matriculate to the best colleges in the world? Looking to earn some pocket money? Answer questions, explain concepts and clarify problems for students wherever and whenever and earn up to $30/hour. Provide quality, intelligent responses to students who strive to achieve academic excellence whilst earning money.
